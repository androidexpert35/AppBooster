🚀 Make every app on your phone faster — with one tap.

OptiDroid uses Android's built-in ART compiler to re-optimize all your installed apps for maximum performance. The result? Faster app launches, smoother scrolling, and a snappier device — without needing root access.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

HOW IT WORKS

Android compiles apps in the background over time, but it doesn't always do the best job. Many apps remain partially optimized or fall back to interpreted mode after updates. OptiDroid fixes this by running Android's own dex2oat compiler with aggressive settings on every app that needs it.

① Analyze — Smart scan detects which apps need optimization
② Optimize — One tap re-compiles everything with your chosen mode
③ Enjoy — Faster launches, smoother UI, better battery efficiency

It's like defragmenting your phone's app layer. Safe, effective, and built on the same tools Android uses internally.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

✨ KEY FEATURES

🔍 Smart Analysis
• Scans all installed apps to find what needs optimization
• Intelligent skip logic — already-optimized apps are automatically skipped
• Shows detailed per-app compilation status and history

⚡ One-Tap Batch Optimization
• Optimize all apps at once with a single tap
• Real-time progress with per-app status updates
• Live activity feed so you can watch it work
• Runs in the background — use your phone while it works

🎛️ Two Optimization Modes
• Speed Profile — Profile-guided, balanced for daily use
• Full Optimization — Maximum performance, compiles everything

🔐 No Root Required
• Uses Shizuku for safe, privileged access

🎨 Beautiful Modern UI

🔒 PRIVACY & SAFETY

OptiDroid does NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal data. Everything happens locally on your device. No analytics, no ads, no tracking — ever.

The app only uses official Android compilation commands (cmd package compile). It does not modify app data, system files, or anything beyond the compilation cache. Completely safe and reversible.
